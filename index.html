My Vision: Curiosity

In a world that often equates success with inherent intelligence, I embrace a different perspective - one deeply rooted in the belief that curiosity is the key. The essence of expertise and growth is not predetermined by some capacity for knowledge, but in the relentless pursuit of understanding driven by an insatiable curiosity.

In Albert Einstein's words: 'Everyone is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will live its whole life believing that it is stupid.' This quote resonates with my core belief that traditional measures of intelligence cannot encompass each individual's diverse and unique strengths.
